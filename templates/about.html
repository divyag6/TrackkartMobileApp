About Trackkart..

This app aims to provide a consolidated platform to track orders placed through various e-commerce websites in India.

As a start, it covers 6 popularly used e-commerce vendors, which are 'Amazon' , 'Flipkart' , 'Snapdeal' , 'Pepperfry' , 'Fabfurnish' and 'Jabong'.

In future, we hope to add more vendors.Happy Tracking!!
